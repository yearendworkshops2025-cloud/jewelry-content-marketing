1

Define Your Brand Voice

Your brand voice is your shop's personality in writing. Before creating content, let's discover what makes your jewelry business unique.

AI Prompt

Discover Your Brand Personality

Help me define my jewelry brand voice. My shop specializes in [handcrafted/vintage/modern/luxury] jewelry. My typical customer is [describe: age, style, occasion]. I want my brand to feel [3 words: e.g., elegant, approachable, sophisticated]. What tone and style should my content have?
AI Prompt

Create Brand Guidelines

Based on my jewelry brand that is [describe style], create a simple brand voice guide including: 5 words we always use, 5 words we never use, our tone (formal/casual), and 3 example sentences in our brand voice.

Pro Tip

Save your brand voice guide and reference it every time you create content. Consistency builds recognition and trust with your customers.

2

Product Descriptions That Sparkle

Transform basic product details into compelling stories that connect emotionally with buyers and help them imagine wearing your pieces.

Before

"Gold necklace, 18 inches, heart pendant. 14k gold. Lobster clasp."

After

"A whisper of romance: This delicate 18-inch chain cradles a heart pendant that catches the light with every movement. Crafted in warm 14k gold, it's the perfect way to keep love close - whether it's a gift for someone special or a treasure for yourself."

AI Prompt

Write Compelling Product Descriptions

Write a product description for my [piece type: ring/necklace/bracelet/earrings]. Details: [material, size, gemstones, unique features]. Target customer: [who would buy this]. Occasion: [gift/self-purchase/special event]. Tone: [from my brand voice]. Include the feeling it evokes and why it's special.
AI Prompt

Batch Create Multiple Descriptions

I need to write descriptions for 5 jewelry pieces. For each, I'll give you: name, type, material, and price point. Create descriptions that are 2-3 sentences, emotionally engaging, and highlight what makes each piece special. My brand voice is [your voice]. Here are the pieces: [list them]

Pro Tip

Always mention the feeling the piece evokes - customers buy emotions, not just metal and stones. "Makes you feel confident" sells better than "high-quality craftsmanship."

3

Your 30-Day Content Calendar

Stop wondering "what should I post today?" Build a content calendar around four key pillars that showcase your expertise and keep customers engaged.

💍

Product Spotlights

Showcase individual pieces with stories, details, and styling ideas

🎭

Behind the Scenes

Show your craft, workspace, and the passion behind your creations

👑

Styling Tips

Help customers imagine how to wear and pair your jewelry

💖

Customer Stories

Share testimonials, photos, and the moments your jewelry celebrates

AI Prompt

Generate a Weekly Content Calendar

Create a 7-day social media content calendar for my jewelry shop. Include: Monday (Product Spotlight), Tuesday (Behind the Scenes), Wednesday (Styling Tip), Thursday (Customer Story or Testimonial), Friday (Product Spotlight), Saturday (Fun/Engagement post), Sunday (Week ahead preview). For each day, give me: post idea, caption draft, and 5 relevant hashtags. My brand voice is [your voice] and I specialize in [your specialty].
AI Prompt

Create Seasonal Content

Create 10 content ideas for [upcoming season/holiday: Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Holiday Season, etc.] for my jewelry shop. Include a mix of promotional posts, gift guides, and emotional storytelling. Each idea should have a hook, main message, and call-to-action.

Pro Tip

Batch your content creation! Spend 2 hours once a week generating all your posts, then schedule them. This saves more time than creating daily.

4

Email Marketing Made Easy

Email is your direct line to customers who want to hear from you. Set up automated sequences that nurture relationships and drive sales while you sleep.

👋 Welcome Series

Greet new subscribers, share your story, and offer a first-purchase incentive

🎉 Product Launches

Build anticipation, reveal new pieces, and create urgency

🍃 Seasonal Collections

Highlight pieces perfect for upcoming holidays and seasons

💝 Re-engagement

Win back customers who haven't purchased in a while

AI Prompt

Create a Welcome Email Series

Write a 3-email welcome series for new subscribers to my jewelry shop newsletter. Email 1 (Day 1): Welcome and brand story. Email 2 (Day 3): Showcase bestsellers and what makes us unique. Email 3 (Day 5): Special offer for first purchase. My brand is [describe] and my voice is [your brand voice]. Include subject lines that get opened.
AI Prompt

Generate Subject Lines That Convert

Generate 10 email subject lines for my jewelry shop for [purpose: new collection launch / holiday sale / back in stock / abandoned cart]. They should be: under 50 characters, create curiosity or urgency, match my brand voice [describe], and avoid spam trigger words. Give me both subtle/elegant and bold/direct options.

Pro Tip

Your subject line determines whether your email gets opened. Test different styles (questions, emojis, personalization) and track what works for your audience.

💬

🔗

Conversions

New followers & subscribers

AI Prompt

Analyze Your Content Performance

Help me analyze my content performance. My top 3 performing posts were about [topics] and got [engagement numbers]. My lowest performing posts were about [topics] with [numbers]. What patterns do you see? What should I do more of? What should I change? Give me 5 specific recommendations.
AI Prompt

Monthly Content Review

I'm doing my monthly content review for my jewelry shop. This month I posted [number] times across [platforms]. My goals were [list goals]. Here are my results: [share metrics]. Help me identify: What worked well, what to improve, and create 3 experiments to try next month.

Pro Tip

Review your analytics weekly, but only make strategic changes monthly. Give your content time to work before pivoting.
