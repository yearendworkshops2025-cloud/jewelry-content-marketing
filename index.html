1

Fabricate a Personality

Your "brand voice" is the lie you tell consistently. It's not really you - you're exhausted and dead inside. But customers don't want that. They want sparkle.

AI Prompt

Generate a Fake Personality

Help me create a brand personality that hides my crippling anxiety. My shop sells [overpriced/reasonably priced/suspiciously cheap] jewelry. My typical customer has [more money than sense/excellent taste/a shopping addiction]. I want my brand to feel [authentic but not too authentic/luxurious but affordable/unique like everyone else]. What lies should I tell consistently?
AI Prompt

Create Your Mask Guidelines

Based on my jewelry brand that pretends to be [describe fantasy], create a guide including: 5 buzzwords to overuse, 5 honest words to never say (like "markup" or "desperate"), our tone (fake casual/corporate warm), and 3 example sentences that sound human but were written by a machine.

Dark Truth

Your "authentic voice" is a marketing construct. But so is everyone else's. At least the AI won't have an existential crisis about it at 3am.

2

Emotional Manipulation 101

Turn "it's a necklace" into "this will fill the void in your soul" (it won't, but they'll buy it anyway).

Honest Version

"Gold necklace, 18 inches, heart pendant. 14k gold. Lobster clasp. You probably don't need this."

Manipulative Version

"A whisper of romance for your empty life: This delicate chain cradles a heart pendant that catches the light with every desperate movement. Crafted in warm 14k gold, it's the perfect way to feel something - anything - whether it's retail therapy or a guilt gift for someone you've neglected."

AI Prompt

Generate Emotional Manipulation

Write a product description for my [shiny thing]. Details: [it's metal with rocks on it]. Target customer: [someone with disposable income and emotional needs]. Occasion: [guilt/self-pity/showing off]. Tone: [manipulatively warm]. Include feelings it won't actually evoke but sounds good, and reasons they definitely need this despite having 47 similar items.
AI Prompt

Mass Produce the Lies

I need to write descriptions for 5 jewelry pieces that are basically the same but with different prices. For each, create unique-sounding descriptions that hide the fact that they're all mass-produced with a 400% markup. Make each sound like a "limited treasure" even though I have 200 in stock. My brand voice is [desperately cheerful].

Dark Truth

Customers buy emotions, not products. You're not selling metal - you're selling the brief dopamine hit of retail therapy. The piece won't change their life. But don't tell them that.

Create a 7-day content calendar for my jewelry shop that I'll resent making. Create 10 content ideas exploiting [upcoming holiday that has nothing to do with jewelry]. Include: guilt-trip gift guides, manufactured urgency, and emotional manipulation timed perfectly with people's insecurities. Each idea should prey on a specific fear: being alone, being forgotten, not being loved enough. Make it sound wholesome.

Dark Truth

Batch your content creation so you only have to feel dead inside once a week instead of daily. Efficiency in suffering.

4

Inbox Invasion Tactics

Email is your direct line to inboxes that will ignore you. Set up automated spam that nurtures resentment while you sleep.

👋 Love Bombing Series

Overwhelm new subscribers immediately so they regret signing up for that 10% discount

🚨 Fake Urgency Launches

"LIMITED TIME ONLY!" (It's always available, we just want you to panic-buy)

🎃 Holiday Guilt Trips

"Don't you love your mother? Buy her this or she'll know you forgot"

👻 Zombie Resurrection

"We miss you!" No we don't. We miss your money. Please come back.

AI Prompt

Create a Love Bombing Sequence

Write a 3-email welcome series that slowly erodes subscriber resistance. Email 1 (Day 1): "Yay you joined!" with our manufactured origin story. Email 2 (Day 3): "Look how special we are" with items they can't afford. Email 3 (Day 5): The guilt trip discount - "We noticed you didn't buy anything..." My brand is [pretending to be small and struggling] and my voice is [desperately friendly]. Include subject lines that create anxiety.
AI Prompt

Generate Clickbait Subject Lines

Generate 10 email subject lines that manipulate people into opening. They should: create FOMO, imply scarcity that doesn't exist, use psychological tricks like "Don't open this" or "I made a mistake", and make people feel bad for not buying. Include at least 3 that exploit insecurity about gift-giving or being a good partner/parent.

Dark Truth

Your subject line is a tiny manipulation. The whole game is tricking people into opening an ad. We're all complicit in the attention economy. Anyway, use emojis.
